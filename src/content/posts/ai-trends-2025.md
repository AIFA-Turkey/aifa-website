The landscape of Artificial Intelligence is evolving at a breakneck pace. As we step into 2025, several key trends are emerging that will shape the future of business and technology in Turkey and beyond.

## 1. Agentic AI
Gone are the days of simple chatbots. Agentic AI systems are capable of planning, reasoning, and executing complex tasks autonomously. These agents act as digital employees, handling everything from customer support workflows to data analysis pipelines with minimal human intervention.

## 2. Hyper-Personalization
Businesses are leveraging AI to deliver hyper-personalized experiences. Whether it's retail recommendations or tailored financial advice, AI models are now analyzing vast datasets to understand individual user preferences better than ever before.

## 4. Multimodal Capabilities
AI is becoming truly multimodal, seamlessly processing and generating text, images, audio, and video. This convergence opens up new possibilities for creative industries and accessibility tools.

## 5. AI Governance and Ethics
As AI becomes ubiquitous, the focus on responsible AI is sharpening. Frameworks for ethical ensuring transparency, fairness, and accountability are becoming standard requirements for enterprise deployment.
